A Warkworth Local Institution

For years, K & J Takeaways has been a trusted cornerstone of the Warkworth community. We pride ourselves on offering a diverse menu that perfectly balances authentic Chinese cuisine with classic European takeaway favourites.

Whether you are craving a comforting bowl of sweet and sour pork, a classic Kiwi burger, or catering for a large family gathering, our focus is always on delivering top-quality food with warm, friendly service.
